Some of My Favorite Games

Intro

I've been gaming since the days of the NES and Windows 3.11/MS-DOS. This early exposure sparked my interest in computers. While it's tough to pick an all-time favorite, what follows are a few games that really stand out to me.

Outer Wilds

Outer Wilds is a unique adventure puzzle game. The less you know going in the better. One thing I love about it is there are no arbitrary checkpoints, inventory, or point progress. All progress happens in your own mind.

Spiritfarer

This is the most emotional game I've ever experienced. I would have never tried it but it was on the Xbox Gamepass and I was hooked right away. Instead of being typical power fantasy game, it's more about acting as an end of life caretaker for lost spirits who passed away and helping them to finally pass on to the afterlife.

VTOL VR

VTOL VR is a near future VR combat flight simulator. It's the perfect balance between an accessable action game and a highly realistic flight simulator. Designed to be fully interactive in VR, there's no need for hardware controls (execpt for maybe a foot rudder).

Outro

My passion for gaming convinced me to learn about game development as a hobby and to help me prepare to move into the tech field.
